About

Hi, I'm Eliza. I was born and raised in Portland but spent the past several years living in New York City. For the past decade I've worked in film in Wardrobe and Art Department, mostly doing commercials, tv series and music videos. I love to make things. I've decided to take a class in coding because I've always been interested in it and felt like self-directed learning wasn't working for me. I'm interested in interactive art and how coding can be used to create interactive experiences.

I'm a classically trained pianist and have played in multiple bands over the years. I can also play guitar and a little bit of bass. Currently, I'm learning how to play the drums. I like to dig for records and I DJ under the name Womb Service. I have a show I host on xray.fm (107.1fm and 91.1fm, xray.fm on the web) every other Monday from 3pm to 4pm. I'm prerecording shows for the duration of this class, however. Often I have DJ nights at places in town. I'm working on my own songs in Ableton but miss playing with a band.

I earned my BFA in Intermedia at PNCA in 2008. I really enjoy cooking and am a little obsessed with Bon Appetit's recipes and kitchen gadgets in general. Some recipes I enjoy making and eating are:

  1. Raspberry Ricotta Cake
  2. Rigatoni with Easy Vodka Sauce
  3. Momofuku's Roasted Brussels Sprouts with Fish Sauce Vinaigrette
  4. Pasta with Mushrooms and Prosciutto
  5. Chicken and Broccoli Stir Fry

I play soccer on the weekends and take barre classes; I'm hoping the gym in the building will help me have more stamina as a runner. I have a lot of trouble falling asleep so exercising helps. I'm not a morning person and hoping I won't have too many issues with waking up in time for class!
